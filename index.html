Messier project

The Messier catalogue is the first list of deep sky objects created in the 18th-century in France by Charles Messier, an astronomer from the french navy.Originaly Charles Messier created the list to avoid distracting targets in his serch for comets.

The catalogue that bears his name now contains 110 of the most glorious nebula and galaxies-plus one super nova remain- for amatuer astronomers to hunt.
